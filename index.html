The story of a teacher who became a boss

The life of Walter White, a chemistry teacher, takes an unexpected turn when he faces a personal crisis. In an attempt to secure his family's future, he makes decisions that will have a profound impact on those he loves. As he delves deeper into an increasingly dark world, Walter must fight to keep his family together and protect them from the consequences of his actions.
